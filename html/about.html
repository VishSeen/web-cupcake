Meet
Emily Montanna

ABOUT

It all started with baking cupcakes for her friends and friends of friends. With their unconditional support, Emily decided to fully devote herself into the art and became an expert. Her cupcake shop was home-based but as she time went by, she opened her first shop which is located in Quatre-Bornes – in 2005. Since then, she has always been experimenting with new flavours and combinations to serve the best cupcakes in town.
They are filled, piped, drizzled and swirled by hand using only the finest ingredients available locally. She takes great pride for her commitment to excellent customer service and the wide variety of cupcakes that she offers.
